How to Export a Video for TikTok or Instagram

Learn how to export your completed video using settings that work well for TikTok and Instagram. Exporting your video correctly helps maintain video quality and ensures it displays properly on social media platforms.

Before starting this task, complete all editing tasks and review your project to make sure it is ready for publishing.
Exporting converts your project into a finished video file that can be shared on social media. Choosing the appropriate export settings helps maintain video quality and ensures your content displays correctly on TikTok and Instagram.
  1. Open your completed CapCut project.
    Review your video one final time before exporting.
    The completed project appears in the editing workspace.
  2. Tap the Export button in the upper-right corner of the screen.
    The Export button is represented by an upward arrow icon.
    The export settings screen appears.
  3. Select a resolution of 1080p.
    A resolution of 1080p provides high-quality video while remaining compatible with most social media platforms.
    The export resolution is updated.
  4. Select a frame rate of 30 frames per second (fps).
    Thirty fps provides smooth playback and is commonly used for TikTok and Instagram videos.
    The frame rate is updated.
  5. Verify that your video uses a vertical 9:16 aspect ratio.
    TikTok and Instagram Reels are optimized for vertical videos.
    The video is ready for mobile viewing.
  6. Tap Export.
    Exporting may take several seconds depending on video length and device performance.
    CapCut begins processing the video.
  7. Wait for the export process to finish.
    Avoid closing the application while exporting.
    The video is saved to your device.
  8. Select TikTok or Instagram if you want to share the video immediately.
    • Tap TikTok to upload directly to TikTok.
    • Tap Instagram to upload to Instagram Reels.
    • Close the sharing screen if you plan to post later.
    The selected social media application opens.
You have successfully exported your video from CapCut. Your video is now saved to your device and ready to be shared on TikTok, Instagram, or other social media platforms.
Your video is ready to be uploaded to TikTok, Instagram Reels, or other social media platforms.
