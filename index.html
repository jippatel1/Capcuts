How to Use This Guide to Edit Videos in CapCut

This is a beginner-friendly guide that explains how to create short social media videos using CapCut on an iPhone. These instructions are designed for college students with little to no editing experience.

Purpose of This Guide

This guide teaches beginner users how to create social media videos in CapCut through a series of step-by-step topics. Users will learn how to create a project, organize clips, add text and music, apply transitions, and export videos for social media platforms like TikTok or Instagram.

Who This Guide Is for

• Beginner college students with little or no editing experience

• Users creating content for TikTok or Instagram Reels

• Student organizations creating promotional content

• Intermediate users who want a quick review of editing basics

What You Will Need

• iPhone or iPad

• CapCut app installed

• Video clips or photos

• Internet connection (recommended)

Estimated Time

Task Time
Starting a Project 1-2 minutes
Editing clips 5-10 minutes
Adding Text/Music 5-10 minutes
Exporting 2-3 minutes
